What is zero waste?

  • It means trying not to throw away any trash.

  • Reduce, reuse and recycle everything possible.

  • An average person produces over 300 kg of waste per year.

  • Plastic takes hundreds of years to decompose.

  • A bottle can be recycled and become a new bottle.

  • Many people use cloth bags instead of plastic ones.

  • Food scraps can become fertilizer for plants (compost).

  • The less waste there is, the cleaner the planet is.
