Terms of the game "Recycling Mission"

In this game you will learn how to recycle waste.

  • Start the game by clicking on the image on the right!

  • Use the left and right arrows on the keyboard to move the waste to the correct containers;

  • You win the game when you get 10 points;

  • You lose when you run out of lives;
